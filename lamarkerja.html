List of applied vacancies

The "List of Applied Vacancies" provides a comprehensive overview of all the job positions I have applied for. This list includes detailed information such as the job title, company name, date of application, and the status of each application. It serves as a record to track my job search progress and ensure that I am organized and prepared for any follow-up communications or interviews. By maintaining this list, I can effectively manage my job applications and stay informed about the opportunities I am pursuing.
